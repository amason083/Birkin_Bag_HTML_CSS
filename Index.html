Top 5 Birkin Bags 2023

These are my top bag picks for the year of 2023 of the Hermes Collection: Birkin.

No 5

Hermès Togo Birkin 25 Bag

This one takes the place of number 5 for it's classic color and design. The brown is guaranteed to go with almost all outfits and the silver accents does not overtake the design yet still makes a statement. This one sits at five an not a higher ranking for the same reason for all its praises, it is safe.

No 4

Hermès Birkin 35 Gris Pale Togo Gold Hardware

This one is number 4 for it's beautiful, pearl color with gold accents. Whether you are out for a night on the town or shopping for even more birkin bags, this will guarantee to get heads to turn and match with almost anything but be careful of blue jeans.

No 3

Hermès Birkin 30 Blue Jean Togo Gold Hardware

This one is number 4 for it's vibrant color and standout stitching. It will be a perfect statement piece and will match with your favorite blue jeans. Ranking high but there is more options to come.

No 2

Hermès Birkin 25 Gris Neve Matte Alligator Palladium Hardware

Coming in as the runner-up, this birkin bag manages to pull off grace when it comes to an uncommon material in high-fashion, Alligator skin. Yet, of course, Hermes pulls it off. The material is subtle and I think this specific color is what pulls it off.

No 1

Hermès Birkin 30 Vert Yucca Togo Palladium Hardware

Coming in at number one is this spring-ready birkin bag. With stiching matching the leather, this bag wins out with its inviting, life-bringing color white still instilling a subtle piece. With silver accebts, it is the perfect piece.
